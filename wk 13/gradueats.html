GraduEats

ABOUT

GraduEats is a brand I co-created for a Digital Marketing class in Leeds. GraduEats is a blog with easy, step by step recipes for college students, like myself. We had to create a website, and drive traffic to this site through numerous channels. We created a Youtube channel and TikTok profile which drew quite a bit of attention for our site. In this project, I used Weebly for site design, TikTok, Youtube, Google Analytics, Google Ads, and search engine optimization techniques. The site is no longer active, as we only paid for a Weebly site for the duration of our class, but photos of the site are below.
