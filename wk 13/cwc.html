Colorado Women's Center

ABOUT

The Colorado Women’s Center is a mental health and therapy organization centered around women’s empowerment. From May to September 2021, I served as their social media intern, and planned and executed posts for their social media platforms. I continued posting on their existing Instagram, and started a new TikTok profile for the company. For this job I used Instagram, TikTok, Microsoft Excel (for planning/scheduling posts), Canva, and Adobe Lightroom.
